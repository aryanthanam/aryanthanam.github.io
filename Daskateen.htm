Description

duskateen was made for a student game jan organised by Swissnex India where students from various colleges from India and student from ZHDK and ECAL Switzerland team up for a 24 hour game jam.

duskateen is a party game where players have to shout into their mobile phones to attarct various customers in the market. The orientation of the mobile controls the direction of the shout in game and loudness controlls the distance of the shout
