Description

Can't See/Hear/Speak is an experimental game where the players have to use their limited form of communication to help each other pass through the level.All three players embody a character affected with one of the title's impairment. Both the Deaf and the Mute are in the same level while the Blind is in a remote location holding clues for them to progress. The key lies in a unique triangle of communication where the Blind can talk to the Mute while receiving only the Deaf's messages
