Description

Tiny Little Intruder is comic themed 2D point and click adventure game, in which the player embodies a tiny alien character. The tiny alien is on a mission to erase valued data from earth, which was transmitted from it's planet. The story proceeds on how the player helps the alien to crafts items, interacts with objects, solve puzzles.
